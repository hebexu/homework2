About me

Hebe Xu was born on the Feburary 18, 1981 in the city of Miluo, Hunan Province, China. When she was 22, she moved to Shanghai City for working and living after she graduated from the university. She married with Fei Yan, now they have 2 kids, one boy is almost 10 years old and one girl is almost 4 years old. When she was young, she wanted to be a teacher. She studied very hard and was accepted into a Bachelor of Computer Science and Technology at Hunan University.

She worked as an IT technical specialist in Shanghai since she graduated from the university in 2002. She turned to her professions from technical into sales and marketing field in IT area when she worked for intel channel department in 2006. After that, she spent 8 years on partner management, channel management and cloud business development in IT field till 2014. However, she soon realized that she needed further learning to improve her professional knowledge. She decided to go to United Kingdom for pursuing her study of MBA course in 2014 and worked as a SaaS (Software -as-a-Service) business development manager in Beijing for 5 years after she came back from UK. She was rewarded as “the best employee”, “company winner” and “top talent” when she worked in IT companies in the past 17 years.

Considering to giving the children a better education and multi-culture living environment, Hebe and her family moved to Adelaide from Beijing in July, 2019. During this time, she became interested in Yoga and spent one year to join in the professional yoga tutor training and certification. Hebe achieved her dream in 2019 and became the certified Yoga Tutor in Adelaide, Australia. She has been training over 80 people to practice Yoga asanas, pranayama and meditations in Adelaide in her own yoga studio since September 2019. But now she is also proactive for looking for an IT professional job, because she wants to continue to grow her career in her strength IT area.
